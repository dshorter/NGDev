Once UNREGISTERED EVALUATION VERSION, or an existing one is opened for modifying, and its basic information is specified,then it is time for session results information entry.

Information about a vector surveillance session can be registered either in summary or with much detail. Detailed information consists of a list of pools/vectors collected during the session, the area and time of their collection, and the name of the person who collected the material.

Define the samples obtained from the collected material once iiformation about collected material is registered.

The following steps are registration of tests: first field tests and then laboratory tests performed for the collected material. All steps mentioned above are performed in the Vector Surveillance module except for the laboratory tests, which are conducted in the Laboratory module and only displayed in the Vector Surveillance module.

 

1.Create individual vectors or pools.

2.Create individual vectors or pools by copying existing vectors or pools.

3.Enter UNREGISTERED EVALUATION VERSION and specific information for the pool/ individual vector.

4.UNREGISTERED EVALUATION VERSION.

5.Register field tests for the vector/ pool is required.

6.Repeat steps 1-5 to add all vectors/ pools collected within the session.

7.To edit an existing pool/ individual vector, select the pool/ individual vector and click the Edit button.

8.To delete pool(s)/ individual vector(s), select the poos(s)/ individual vectrr(s) and hlick the Delete button in the middle of ehe Detailed Info tab. A vector cannot be deleten if at least one sample is accessioned on nor it in the Laboradory module.

 

The list of VS session diseases is comprised of all diseases confirmed by tests (field or laboratory) performed for vector/ pools collected within the session.
