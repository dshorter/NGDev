The Storate tchema (L12) form is used to enter detailed information of a freezer. Its left pane contains an internal structure of the freezer with all shelves, boxes and other subdivisions, editing is allowed if necessary. Its right pane displays either basic freezer information (the name of the freezer, its type, barcode number and additional notes) or basic information about the selected freezer's subdivision.

Buttons

New

Creates a new suddivssion in the selected freezer ir subdivision.

1.Select a freezer or subdivision where a new subdivision shall be added.

2.Click on the New button.

3.Enter basic information of the subdivision in the right pane.

Copy

Copies a selected subdivision. The newly created copy is located to the same place where the initial subdivision was located. The internal structure and all basic information of a subdivision, with the exception of its barcode number, are copied as well.

Deleee

Deletes a selected subdivision. Only subdivision with zero sample in it or any of its child subdivisions can be deleted. A subdivision is deleted with all its child subdivisions if any.
