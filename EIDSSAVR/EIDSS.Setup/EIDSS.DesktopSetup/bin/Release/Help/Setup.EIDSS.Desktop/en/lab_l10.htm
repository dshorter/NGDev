Sample Transfer L10

The Sample Transfer (L10) form is used to transfer samples in and out of the site laboratory.

Fig. 493 Sample Transfer form (Transfer In)

Data Fields

Sample Transfer ID

Identifier of a transfer in or out operation.

•For a transfer out operation, the Sample Transfer ID field is populated automatically according to the numbering scheme and can be manually changed until the package is transferred out.

•For a transfer in operation, an identifier assigned by the package sender is displayed and cannot be changed.

Purpose of the transfer

Enter the purpose of the transfer out. The field is available until the operation is finished.

Transfer From

The transfer source organization cannot be changed:

•for a transfer out, the current organization is displayed

•for a transfer in, the external organization, which sent the package, is displayed.

Sent by

The sender of the package cannot be changed:

•for a transfer out, the current user shall be displayed after the transfer operation is finished.

•for a transfer in, the employee of the external organization, who sent the package, is displayed.

Transfer to

Enter a receiving organization. For transfer out only.

Date sent

The transfer date cannot be changed.

•for a transfer out, the current date shall be displayed after the transfer operation is finished.

•for a transfer in, the date when the package left the source organization is displayed.

Columns

Source Lab ID

The initial lab sample ID:

•for a transfer out, the current lab sample ID is used.

•for a transfer in, the lab sample ID used in the external organization is used.

Sample Type

The type of a sample.

Date Received

The date when the sample was received; can be manually changed until the form is saved. For a transfer in only.

Received By

The current user name is displayed as an employee who received the sample. For a transfer in only.

Lab Sample ID

A lab sample ID newly created  in accordance with the numbering scheme for transferred in samples and can be manually changed. Not applicable for a transfer out.

Sample Condition

Enter the condition of the transferred in samples. If a sample is accepted in a poor condition status or rejected, an explanation is required in the Comment cell. The cell becomes available once the Transfer In button is clicked, and has to be specified before the form is saved for the first time. After that the cell becomes unavailable.

Comment

Enter a comment for transferred in samples. If a sample is accepted in a poor condition or rejected, the Comment cell become mandatory for an explanation.  The cell becomes available once the Transfer In button is clicked, and has to be specified before the form is first time saved. Afterwards the cell becomes unavailable.

Storage Location

Assign a location in the storage for a transferred in sample. The cell becomes available once the Transfer In button is clicked, and has to be specified before the form is saved for the first time. Afterwards the cell becomes unavailable.

1.Click on the Details button (). The Freezers window opens.

2.Select the desired location.

3.Click on the OK button. The Freezers window closes. The assigned location is displayed in the cell.

Functional Area

Select the department of the organization to receive the transferred in sample from a drop-down list.

The cell becomes available once the Transfer In button is clicked, and has to be specified before the form is saved for the first time. Afterwards the cell becomes unavailable.

Buttons

Transfer In

Marks all samples in the list for a transfer in and makes them available for changes (see the description of the Columns above).

1.Click on the Transfer In button.

2.Change the data in the columns.

3.Save the form. For detailed description of a transfer in operation, read the Transfer in a sample section.

Transfer Out

Marks all samples in the list for a transfer out and makes them available for changes. See the description of the Columns above.

1.Specify a desired organization for them.

2.Click on the Transfer Out button.

3.Change the data in the columns.

4.Save the form. For a detailed description of a transfer out operation, read the Transfer out a sample (IF) section.

The button becomes available after the destination organization is specified.

Add

Opens the Samples List L01 form to select those samples that shall be added to the package.

Delete

Deletes selected samples from the transfer package.

OK

Saves the data in the form and confirms the transfer operation.

Cancel

Cancels all changes made in the form and the transfer operation.
