Transfer out sample from drop-down menu (LS)

1.From the Journals menu, select Laboratory Section. The Laboratory Section window opens.

2.Find and select sample(s) to be transferred out.

3.Right-click them and from the drop-down menu select Transfer out. The Transfer Out window opens.

Fig. 466 Transfer Out  window

4.Select to which organization the package shall be sent.

5.Select the date of the transfer from the calendar.

6.Click the OK button to confirm the transfer out operation. The Transfer Out window closes. The sample(s) status changes to Transferred Out.

7.Click the Save button and confirm the saving.
