Pop-up Notifications

The Alerts are messages that inform about important events.

Fig. 40  Replication alert message

 

Opening the list of the alerts:

Click the message. The Site Alerts Messenger window opens.

Fig. 41  List of replication actions

Viewing the detailed information about an event:

Select it in the list and click the View button. The detailed form of the event is opened in read-only mode.
