Print paper forms for a veterinary case

Show/Hide Hidden Text

Paper forms for the veterinary cases can be printed and used as a physical source of information. There are two types of veterinary paper forms: case investigation form and case tests form.

Case investigation form

The case investigation form is the general paper form of the veterinary case, and it contains similar information of the form tabs. Click Paper Forms in the veterinary form window to open the paper form. Please refer to the Paper Forms section for complete information on the paper form interface.

Fig. 266 Case Investigation Form

Case tests form

The case tests form holds the information about the laboratory tests of the veterinary case. Click Paper Forms in the Lab Tests of the veterinary form window to open the paper form. Please refer to Paper Forms section for complete information on the paper form interface.
