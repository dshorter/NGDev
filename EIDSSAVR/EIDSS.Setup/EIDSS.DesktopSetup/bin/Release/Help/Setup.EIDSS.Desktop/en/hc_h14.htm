Diagnosis History H14

The Diagnosis History (H14) form is opened by clicking the button in the Changed diagnosis (if known) field of the Human Case Report Form.

Fig. 233 Diagnosis Change form

Note: the Diagnosis History form can only be opened if such history exists. Otherwise the following window will appear:

Fig. 234 Diagnosis Change form

The form contains the following parameters:

Change Date/Time

The date and time when the diagnosis was changed.

Person

The user who has performed the diagnosis change.

Organization

The organization of the Person.

Previous Diagnosis

Previous diagnosis if any.

Changed Diagnosis

New diagnosis after the Change Diagnosis procedure.

Reason

The reason for the diagnosis change.
