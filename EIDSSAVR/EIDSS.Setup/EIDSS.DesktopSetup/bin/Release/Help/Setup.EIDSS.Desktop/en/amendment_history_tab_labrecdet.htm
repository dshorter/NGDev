Amendment History tab of Laboratory Record Detailed form

The Amendment History tab is used to display information of each result change of a test.

 

Fig. 508 Amendation History tab

Amendment Date

The date of the amendment.

Amended By (Person)

The name of the person who amended the test results.

Amended By (Organization)

The name of the organization where the test was amended.

Changed Test Result

The new test result value.

Reason of Amendment

The reason of the amendment.
