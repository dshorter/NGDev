Paper forms from human cases can be printed and used as a physical source of information. There are three types of human paper forms: Case Investigation Form, Urgent Notification Form and CasF Tests Form.

 

Case Investigation Form

Case investigation form is the general paper form of the human case, and it contains the similar information of the form tabs. Click Paper Forms in the human form window to open the paper form. Please refer to the Paper Forms section for complete information on the paper form interface.
