Organizations

Show/Hide Hidden Text

All the organizations (facilities) involved in case investigation in human, veterinary and vector surveillance are recorded in the EIDSS database. While working with the list of organizations, avoid making extra entries for organizations already in the system: redundant entries may affect the results of data analysis and search.

       Create an organization in the system:

1.In the System menu, click Administration and then click Organizations. The Organizations List opens.

Fig. 129 Organizations List

2.Click the New button. The Organization Details window opens.

Fig. 130 Information about organization

3.Enter the short name, full name and additional information about the organization. Short name is used during logon to the system.

4.Click Departments tab. Departments tab opens.

Fig. 131 Organization departments

5.Enter the departments, divisions, offices etc. of the organization. To accomplish this, click the upper line of the box, and enter the name of the department. Click the Add button and continue with entering the departments.

6.When the entry of the departments is completed, click the Ok button. The Confirmation window opens.

7.Click Yes to confirm the entry of the organization.

8.Click Close to close the Organizations List window.

       Edit an organization in the system:

1.In the System menu, click Administration and then click Organizations. The Organizations List opens.

Fig. 132 Organizations List

2.Select the organization to edit. Click the Edit button. The Organization Details window opens.

Fig. 133 Information about organization

3.Modify the short name, full name and/or additional information about the organization. The short name is used when logging on to the system.

4.Click the Departments tab. The Departments tab opens.

Fig. 134 Organization departments

5.Enter a new department, division, office etc. of the organization. To do so, click the upper line of the box and enter the name of the department. Click the Add button.

6.To delete a department, select it and click the Delete button.

A deleted department is finally removed from the system and becomes unavailable!

7.When the changes are completed, click the Ok button. The Confirmation window opens.

8.Click Yes to confirm.

9.Click Close to close the Organizations List window.

       Delete an organization from the system:

1.In the System menu, click Administration and then click Organizations. The Organizations List opens.

Fig. 135 Organizations List

2.Select the organization to delete. Click the Delete button. The Delete Record window opens.

Fig. 136 Confirm Deletion

A deleted organization will be permanently deleted from the system. The organization's employees will lose the access rights to the system.

3.Click Yes to confirm.

4.Click Close to close the Organizations List window.
