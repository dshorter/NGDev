Basic Syndromic Surveillance Forms List

Show/Hide Hidden Text

Basic Syndromic Surveillance Forms List is a form where all Basic Syndromic Surveillance reports matching the search criteria are listed.

Fig. 430 Basic Syndromic Surveillance list

Search pane section, which contains different fields used for Basic Syndromic Surveillance reports searching.

Fig. 431 Search parameters

Form ID

In the field Form ID field, the Basic Syndromic Surveillance report ID may be specified.

Notification of

Drop-down list where notification of ILI or SARD may be selected.

Name of Hospital/Sentinel Station

Drop-down list where Hospital or Sentinel Station may be selected.

Report Date From/To

Upper and lower Basic Syndromic Surveillance report date range limits.

Date of Symptoms Onset From/To

Upper and lower symptoms onset date range limits.

Last Name

Family name of person included in report.

First Name

First name of person included in report.

Sex

Drop-down options include Male and Female.

Age (years)

Upper and lower age bounds of person included in report.

Region

In the Region field, exact region may be specified.

Rayon

In the Rayon field, exact rayon may be specified.

Town or Village

In the field Town or Village exact settlement may be specified.

Test Result

Drop-down list where Indeterminate, Negative or Positive test result may be selected.

Site

Drop-down list where EIDSS site may be selected.
Search results list section, which displays a list of Basic Syndromic Surveillance Forms matching the entered search criteria.
