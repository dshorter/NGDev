Fig. 425 Basic Syndromic Surveillance Form

The Basic Syndromic Surveillance Form consists of:

General Info section, which displays general information about the report.

Fig. 426 General Info section

Data fields

Form ID

Basic Syndromic Surveillance Form unique ID, auto-populated.

Date Entered

The date the report information was first entered into the system, auto-populated.

Date Last Saved

The date the report information was last saved in the system, auto-populated.

Entered By

Name of the user who created current Basic Syndromic Surveillance report, auto-populated.

Site

Name of the site where current Basic Syndromic Surveillance report was created, auto-populated.

Notification Of

Drop-down list for selecting surveillance type. The list of the fields in the form depends on the option selected in this field.

Name of Hospital/Sentinel Station

Drop-down list includes organizations, provided investigation.

Report Date

Report date. Must be less than or equal to current date.
Contact Information contains information about the patient and location.

Fig. 427 Contact Information section

Data fields

Last Name

Family name of person included in the report.

First Name

First name of person included in the report.

Middle Name

Patronimic name of person included in the report.

Date of Birth

Date of birth of the person included in this report.

Age, years

Total number of years rounded down when the person included in this report was born.

Age, months

Number of months in excess of whole years rounded down since the person included in this report was born.

Sex

Patient's gender.

Region

Drop-down list includes all the regions in the country where the person included in report resides.

Rayon

Drop-down list includes all the rayons in the region selected where the person included in the report resides.

Town or Village

Drop-down list includes all the cities/settlements in the rayon selected where the person included in the report resides.

Street

A free text field for entering street name where the person included in this report resides.

Building/House/Apt.

A free text field for entering house or building number where the person included in this report resides.

Postal Code

A free text field for entering postal code where the person included in the report resides.

Phone Number

A free text field for entering the primary contact telephone number for the person included in the report.

Personal ID

Unique identifying number assigned to person included in report.
Clinical Information section contains additional information about patient’s symptoms. The content of the section depends on the value of the “Notification of” field.

Fig. 428 Clinical Information section

Data fields

Date of symptoms onset

Day, month and year that signs or symptoms of disease first appeared in the person included in the report.

Pregnant

Yes/No/Unknown drop-down list to indicate that the person included in this report was pregnant at the time of the report.

Postpartum period (6 weeks)

Yes/No/Unknown drop-down list to indicate that the person included in this report was within 6 weeks of giving birth at the time of the report. Inactive if the person is pregnant.

Fever > 38°C

Yes/No/Unknown drop-down list to indicate that the person included in the report had a fever equal to or greater that 38 degrees Celsius.

Method of Measurement

Drop-down list to indicate method for measuring body temperature of person included in the report. User selects Oral, Axillary, or Other.

If other, enter method

A free text field to identify a method other than the options provided used to measure the body temperature of the person included in the report.

Cough

Yes/No/Unknown drop-down list to indicate whether clinical signs included coughing.

Shortness of Breath

Yes/No/Unknown drop-down list to indicate whether clinical signs included shortness of breath.

Seasonal Flu Vaccine

Yes/No/Unknown drop-down list to indicate whether a seasonal flu vaccine was received by the person included in the report.

Patient was administered antiviral medication?

Yes/No/Unknown drop-down list to indicate whether the person included in the report had received antiviral medication as part of their treatment.

Name of medication

Free text field to enter the name of the antiviral medication administered to the person included in the report. Active only if patient was administered antiviral medication.

Date received

Date received. Active only if patient was administered antiviral medication.

Concurrent Chronic Diseases or Conditions

Check boxes to indicate other diseases and conditions that may affect the person included in the report. User selects Respiratory System, Asthma, Diabetes, Cardiovascular, and Obesity: BMI >30; not evaluated, Renal, Liver, Neurological/Physiological, Immunodeficiency, and/or Unknown Etiology.
Samples Information section contains information about the collected samples.

Fig. 429 Contact Information section

Data fields

Sample Collection Date

Day, month and year that clinical sample was obtained from the person included in the report.

Sample ID

Unique identification number assigned to the clinical sample obtained from the person included in the report.

Test Result

Pull down options include Positive, Negative and Indeterminate.

Result Date

Day, month and year that the result became known.
