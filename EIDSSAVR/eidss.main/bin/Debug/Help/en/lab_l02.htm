Sample L02

The Sample (L02) form is a form with detailed information about a sample. Use it to view, edit, or print data, specify a functional area for the sample, or assign tests. The sample form contains a set of basic sample data in the General tab; for example, information about tests assigned to the sample in the Tests tab, and information about transfer out operation for a sample with a Transferred Out status in the Sample Transfer tab. Availability of fields depends on the sample's status and access rights.
