Human Case De-duplication

Two or more cases can be registered in the system but all of them are referring to a single Human Case. The De-duplication procedure is required to discard the unnecessary cases.  

1.Click Search -> Human Case De-duplication. The Human Case De-duplication List window opens.

Fig. 192 Human Cases De-duplication List 

2.In the search pane enter the data to filter the cases and find the required one.

Fig. 193 Example of entered selection criteria - all patients till 18 year sold with confirmed diagnosis, entered this year

3.Click the Search button. The cases which meet the selection criteria are displayed in the right pane.

4.Select 2 possible duplicates one at a time and click the Add for de-duplication button. The cases are added to the upper pane.

Fig. 194 2 cases are added for deduplication

5.Click the De-duplicate button under the upper pane. The Human Case De-duplication Details window opens.

Fig. 195 Selection of a survivior through cases comparison

6.Compare the cases using the Notification tab. The same parameters of both cases that are colored in red differs from each other. Please refer to Human Case De-duplication Details section for complete information on the form.

•If they are duplicates, select Survivor for the case which will be kept, and Superseded for the case which will be deleted. Click Ok.

•If they are not duplicates, click Cancel.

7.Use the Samples tab to select which samples of the Superseded case will be added to the samples of the Survivor case. All samples accessioned in Laboratory Module will be automatically joined to the samples of the Survivor case. Non-accessioned samples of the Superseded case may be selected or deselected in addition to the Survivor case.
