Print paper forms for an aggregate case

Paper forms of the veterinary aggregate cases can be printed, and used as a physical source of information.

To open the Veterinary Aggregate Case paper form click Paper Forms in the Veterinary Aggregate Case Details window. Please refer to the Paper Forms section for complete information about the paper form interface.
